Gabi beamed with pride as she presented to me her precious collection which she
kept in a three-tier jewellery box. “Here, Katy, you may take a closer look," she said, “but
don’t touch." It was a warning she often used on her younger brother. "I keep them in as
good a condition as I can,” she added. She laughed after saying it but did not oppose
when I picked up a few ornaments to take a closer look — a miniature silver ball, 3
metallic strip of ribbon and a piece of foll.
